How It Works

1. Capture

Your webcam captures video frames in real-time and sends them for processing.

2. Detect

MediaPipe detects hand landmarks and ensures a valid hand gesture is present.

3. Analyze

MobileNetV2 extracts features from 13 consecutive frames to understand motion.

4. Predict

LSTM neural network processes the sequence and predicts the ASL word being signed.
